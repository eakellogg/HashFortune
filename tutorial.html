HashFortune Tutorial

Howdy! Thank you for playing our game. Until we set up a more thorough tutorial, here is a brief description of how to play HashFortune, which we will try to update regularly.

How to play the game: (please read this section) HashFortune is a pseudo-stock market based on twitter hashtags. We do get real data from Twitter, and update everything accordingly. The general idea is that you can invest points into any hashtags, and the goal is to do so before they get popular. The value of your investment will be updated every hour (when our twitter data is updated). If you buy 1000 of #tbt when 2 other people are invested and it was tweeted 30 times that hour, if the next hour more people have invested in it or it was used more than 30 times, then the value of your investment will go up. Likewise, if it has less investors or was tweeted less times, the value of your investment will go down.


Homepage (click on the icon in the top left corner to get here)

  • Investments: Once you invest in a hashtag, the value of your investment will show up here
  • Trending Hashtags: contains the top hashtags for the past hour. Every hour, we get a lot of tweets/hashtags from twitter, and the numbers show how many times we saw each hashtag in the past hour
  • Leaderboard: This also updates every hour. You can click on someones name to view what they are invested in.
  • Friends: This will soon be able to show which other players you are friends with and their scores

Investments (click on the $ to get here)

  • Uninvested Points - points that you can use to buy into a hashtag
  • Invested points - these are currently invested in a hashtag
  • Net worth - the sum of your invested and uninvested points
  • Hashtag search type in any hashtag, like tbt, and you will be taken to a page where you can invest any amount of your available points, sell some of what you currently have invested in it, and see a graph at the bottom of the page of how popular it has been over time

Portfolio (click on the briefcase to get here) This shows your general stats and the list of your investments.

Friends (click on the generic friends icon to get here) This page will soon have a list of your pending friend requests and a list of your current friends.

Challenges (click on the fist to get here) Challenges will be up and running in the near future, and more information will be available then.

Settings (click on the settings gear) Here you can find the links to the user study, this tutorial, and the logout button.

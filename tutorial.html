HashFortune Tutorial


Howdy! Welcome to HashFortune!

General Instructions

HashFortune is a game based on buying and sellings stocks based on real Twitter hashtags. Our databases pull hashtags from Twitter each hour, giving you up to date info on the latest trends.
The goal of the game is to buy and sell stocks in hashtags. Investing wisely will increase your net worth and help you rise on the leaderboard. The value of a stock increases both when more shares are bought and when the hashtag appears more on Twitter. So the goal is to invest in a hashtag before it gets popular, and sell it at a peak time for maximum profit.


Some Basics

  • Initially, you will start with 5,000 points to invest as you wish.
  • The initial price of a stock in any hashtag is 20, and this will rise and fall based on the number of shares bought and the hashtag's actual popularity on Twitter.
  • Hashtag stock values will vary immediately in response to a change in the number of shares bought. The value will also be updated hourly based on Twitter popularity.

Fun Features

  • Clicking on a username will take you to their portfolio page, which shows their current investments.
  • Clicking on a hashtag name will take you to its specific page with more stock details and investment options.
  • Hovering over a hashtag name will provide a link to see the Twitter page for that hashtag.
  • Challenges are in the works right now, and will be up very soon!

Friends

  • On the friends page, you can search for a user by username or email to view their portfolio page.
  • When viewing a portfolio page, you have the option to add the player as a friend.
  • After they accept the request, their name will show up in your friends table.
  • If someone requests to be your friend, that request will appear in the Friend Requests table on your friends page.
